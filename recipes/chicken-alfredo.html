Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a luscious Italian-American pasta dish featuring tender pieces of chicken served over perfectly cooked pasta, all smothered in a rich and creamy Alfredo sauce.
This velvety sauce, a blend of butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese, creates a decadent coating that clings to the pasta and chicken, resulting in a sumptuous and deeply satisfying meal.
Often seasoned with garlic, salt, and pepper, Chicken Alfredo embodies comfort and indulgence, making it a beloved classic that is occasionally personalized with additional ingredients like vegetables or spices for added depth of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or chicken tenders
  • Fettuccine pasta (or a pasta of your choice)
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan Cheese (Grated)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional seasonings such as nutmeg or parsley (for added flavor and garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare The Chicken
    • Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
    • In a large skillet, heat some butter over medium-high heat.
    • Add the chicken and cook until it's no longer pink in the center and has a golden-brown exterior.
      This usually takes about 4-6 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of the chicken.
      Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  2. Cook The Pasta
    • In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add a generous pinch of salt.
    • Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions until it's al dente (usually 8-10 minutes).
      Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare The Alfredo Sauce
    • In the same skillet you used for the chicken, add more butter and minced garlic.
      Saut√© the garlic for about a minute until it's fragrant but not browned.
    • Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer, stirring frequently.
    • Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese to the cream mixture, stirring constantly until the sauce becomes smooth,
      thick, and the cheese is fully melted.
    • Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
      You can also add a pinch of nutmeg at this stage if desired for extra flavor.
  4. Combine and Serve
    • Slice the cooked chicken into thin strips or bite-sized pieces.
    • Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce and toss to coat the pasta evenly.
    • Add the sliced chicken to the skillet, gently mixing it into the sauce and pasta.
    • Cook for a few more minutes, allowing everything to heat through.
    • Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley and additional grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
